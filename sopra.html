Features Overview

Titus Audio Symphony line, Sopra sport In-ear headphones, designed to stay stationary during intense workouts. Fitted for comfort and long term wear with a microphone, controller, and noise isolation capabilities. Compatible with all Androids, other smartphones, and tablet computers. Uniquely designed with a fin to be best suited for active wear as well as a rotatable tip to allow for adjustment to fit individual comfort.

 

HIGH FIDELITY SOUND

The Sopra’s powerful drivers deliver an engaging stereo bass with rhythmic beats. The treble performance is crystal clear and smooth, and it reproduces excellent spatial aspects of sound.

ADAPTABLE eAR pOSITIONING

The Sopra offers adjustable angles for your individual needs. Just hold the head of the earbud, then rotate its logo section gently to a comfortable angle to fit into your ear.

COMFORTABLE WEARING

The auricle-fitting design and rounded shape creates comfortable placement and the rubber fin helps the earbud to stay place even during intense workouts. Includes 3 pairs of ear-tips with different size to adjust users’ ear.

CONVENIENT cONTROLS

The Sopra comes with a multi-functional control with simple commands. One click: play/pause. Double clicks: next track. Three clicks: previous track.

CLEAR HANDS-FREE CALLING

A convenient built in Omnidirectional microphone with answer and end call functions for easy hands free phone calls. Click once to answer or end the call.

ON-THE-GO PORTABILITY

With a lightweight design and tangle-proof cord, the Sopra provides a flexible and comfortable use for active movement. Its flat cord with tangle-free and sweat-proof features enhances its use during intense workouts and sports.
